SEQUENCE SURFACES

Field Visit & Presentation Workflow

PHASE 1: PRESENTATION EXECUTION

START: Field Visit Begins

Salesman arrives at customer location with sample products and boxes.

↓

Step 1: Select Opening Product

Choose the most visually appealing, eye-catching product from current range. Something that makes the customer say wow—unique, new, different, and saleable.

↓

Step 2: Present Core Laminate Portfolio

Show main laminate offerings. Display samples individually in right manner. Keep interested samples visible, rotate out samples customer is not interested in.

↓

Step 3: Present New Varieties

Showcase latest products launched by Sequence Surfaces that look premium and attractive.

↓

Step 4: Present Commodity Laminates

Present slower-moving or commonly available laminates that round out the range.

↓

Step 5: Present Decorative Products

Present acrylic, gag sheet, louvers, and other decorative product lines.

↓

Step 6: Sample Organization

Once each product box presentation is complete, place samp pop les back into box and set aside neatly. Do not scatter or stack boxes. Keep presentation space clean and professional at all times.

↓

Step 7: Handle Price Questions

If customer asks about pricing during presentation, respond: "Sir, let me finish the presentation and you have a look at all the products. Then we will discuss the prices."

↓
PHASE 2: PRICING & DECISION

Step 8: Present Pricing in Writing

Do not verbally quote prices. Write on paper in format: Catalog Name | Laminate Price | Sample Box Price. List all catalogs customer showed interest in. Hand the slip to customer to review.

↓

DECISION POINT: Customer Response

Customer reviews pricing and decides whether to work with Sequence Surfaces.

✓ CUSTOMER SAYS YES

Proceed to Phase 3: WhatsApp Group Setup and Backend Briefing

✗ CUSTOMER SAYS NO

Lead ends. Document interaction and file for future follow-up strategy (to be implemented later).

↓
PHASE 3: ONBOARDING & BACKEND BRIEFING

Step 9: Create WhatsApp Group

Create WhatsApp group with customer as admin. Add: Dispatch team, Billing team, Accounts team, Billing Manager, CEO, MD.

↓

Step 10: Post Pricing Slip Photo

Post photo of pricing slip showing all selected catalogs with prices in the WhatsApp group.

↓

Step 11: Backend Team Briefing

Brief backend team to send all selected catalogs to customer along with: Customer GST number, Specific contact details, Customer onboarding form.

↓

END: Lead Converted

Customer onboarded. Backend team has complete clarity on all requirements and data to process.
